Every prayer app keeps adding things. We keep removing them. Nida does three things — and does each one with the precision the act of prayer deserves.

01

Reliable

The Athan fires on time. Every time. Offline calculation, validated against reference sources. A minute off can mean a missed prayer.

02

Questions

Things people ask.

Is it really free?

+

Yes. Free forever. No ads, no paywall, no “premium tier”. Once traction grows, we’ll add a voluntary donation model with a public cost dashboard — you’ll know exactly where every dirham goes.

Does it work offline?

+

Yes. Calculation runs 100% on the device. No internet required once installed. Travel to any timezone — it keeps working.

What about my privacy?

+

Nothing leaves your phone. No telemetry, no analytics, no accounts, no ad networks. The safest data is data that never exists on a server.

iOS — when?

+

The iOS build is in progress. Sign up below (your email stays private) to know when it ships.

Why not just use an existing app?

+

Because the dominant prayer apps became ad-heavy super-apps that serve advertisers first. One sold a hundred million Muslims’ GPS data. A prayer app’s only job is to help you pray on time.

Who is behind Nida?

+

A small independent team. No VCs. No investors. No growth targets. Just a commitment to do one thing right.
