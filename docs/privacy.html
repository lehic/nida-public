Privacy Policy

nida collects no personal data, has no tracking, and works entirely offline.

Last updated: April 7, 2026

nida is an Islamic prayer times application developed by Mehdi Mehni ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how nida handles your data.

1. Data We Collect

nida does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no server-side data processing.

2. Device Permissions

nida may request the following device permissions, all processed locally on your device:

PermissionPurposeData sent to servers
LocationCalculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction based on your positionNone — processed on-device only
NotificationsSend local reminders at prayer timesNone — scheduled locally
Exact AlarmsEnsure prayer notifications fire at the precise scheduled timeNone
Boot CompletedReschedule prayer notifications after a device restartNone
VibrationHaptic feedback when the Qibla compass aligns with the direction of MakkahNone

3. Local Storage

nida stores your preferences (selected city, calculation method, language, notification settings) locally on your device using standard platform storage (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS). This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

nida does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

6. Children's Privacy

nida does not collect data from any user, including children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). The app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR, Quebec Law 25, and CCPA), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since nida does not collect any personal data, these rights are inherently respected:

  • Right to access: No personal data is stored on our servers.
  • Right to deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences.
  • Right to portability: No data exists to transfer.
  • Right to object: No data processing occurs.

8. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored on servers, there is no risk of data breach from our side. Your preferences are stored locally on your device, protected by your device's built-in security.

9. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. We retain no data on any server. Local data is removed when you uninstall the app or clear the app's data.

10. International Users

nida is available worldwide. Since no data is collected or transferred, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new effective date. Since we do not collect any data, significant changes are unlikely. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. Contact
