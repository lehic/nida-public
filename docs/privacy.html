Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

nida is an Islamic prayer times app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect — which is none.

Data Collection

nida does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Location

nida may request access to your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Notifications

nida may send local notifications to remind you of prayer times. These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external server.

Storage

Your preferences (selected city, calculation method, language) are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

nida does not use any third-party services that collect user data. The app works entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

nida does not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Since we don't collect data, changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at mehdi.mehni@gmail.com.
